About

    We have different perspectives in life; some may say that life is a wonder while others would say itâ€™s a dangerous one. Either way, one outlet that we can relay our views is by artistic expression.
    Words, colors, mundane things that are supposedly inanimate are instead containing intrinsic characteristics that are personalized into each of us. Meanings that transcend blunt rational thought.
